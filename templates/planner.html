🌱 Why This Matters

🏠

The average American home produces about 7.5 tons of CO₂ per year from energy use alone.

🚗

One ton of CO₂ equals roughly 2,500 miles of driving in a typical car.

🔌

Residential energy use accounts for roughly 20% of all U.S. greenhouse gas emissions.

💡

Many of the highest-impact changes — LEDs, air-drying, cold washes — cost nothing at all.

🌍

If every U.S. household made modest changes, the effect equals removing tens of millions of cars.

💰

These changes reduce emissions and typically lower energy bills — a double dividend.
